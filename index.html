ABOUT SECTION

ABOUT THIS PORTAL I noticed that many students struggle to manage their syllabus—files often get deleted, lost, or are hard to find during exam time. I developed this website to solve that problem and provide a permanent digital home for our academic curriculum. WHY THIS PORTAL? Always Accessible: You no longer need to worry about losing PDFs. Your syllabus is always just a click away whenever you need it. Non-Profit & Student-Led: This is a strictly non-profit initiative built solely for the welfare of my fellow students. Growing With You: While the portal currently focuses on the 4th Semester, it is designed to evolve. As we move into the 5th and 6th Semesters, this portal will be updated with the new syllabus to support us until graduation. A NOTE FROM THE DEVELOPER "As an Economics student, I believe in the principle that 'Trade can make everyone better off.' In this spirit, I am trading my time and effort to provide these resources so that our entire student community can prosper together." Developed by: ....SUBRAT KUMAR RATHA..... If you have any questions or need help regarding this portal, please feel free to ask us. We are always here to help! HOW TO USE THIS PORTAL Follow these simple steps to access your syllabus quickly: Step 1: Click the link to open the portal in your Chrome Browser. Step 2: Enter your Name to enter the portal. Step 3: Select your Subject from the list. Step 4: Choose between MAJOR or MINOR depending on what you need. If you select MAJOR, you will see options for Paper VIII, Paper IX, and Paper X. If you select MINOR, you will see the minor syllabus for that subject immediately. NEW COURSE FOR 4th SEM: In Semester 4, we have a new course that replaces the MDC. You can find this "New Course for 4th Sem" button at the bottom of the subject selection page. IMPORTANT NAVIGATION TIP: Please do not use your mobile phone's back button, as it may close the portal. Instead, always use the "← Back" button provided on the website screen to move between sections. HOW TO ADD TO YOUR HOME SCREEN (Highly Recommended): To access this portal like a mobile app: Open the website in Chrome. Tap the three dots (⋮) in the upper right corner. Select the option "Add to Home Screen." Now you can open your syllabus anytime with just one tap from your mobile home screen!
